Know More About Us

Questions visitors often ask.

Everything here keeps the heart of your original content, but in a more structured and easier-to-browse format.

Located in Bengaluru, India, we are a community of Christians committed to sharing God's love and living according to the Bible. Guided by His grace and the Holy Spirit, we strive to grow in faith, holiness and alignment with God's will.
Jesus Christ: We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, sent to die and be raised for the forgiveness of our sins. Through His sacrifice, we have the hope of eternal life.

God the Father: We believe that God, the Creator of all, is both merciful and just. He is loving and compassionate, yet perfectly righteous in all His ways.

The Holy Spirit: We believe that the Holy Spirit is God, sent to sanctify and empower believers. He is present in the life of every Christian, guiding them to live according to His will.

The Church: We believe that the church is the bride of Christ, set apart to be holy. It is a community of believers called to reflect God's glory and spread His message to the world.

The Lord's Supper: We believe in the importance of partaking in the Lord's Supper every Sunday. It serves as a remembrance of Jesus' sacrifice, a renewal of our bond with Him, and a shared expression of our unity in Christ.

Salvation: We believe that salvation is a gift from God, given by grace through faith in Jesus Christ. It is not earned by works, but by God's mercy, through repentance and baptism (Acts 2:38).

The Bible: We believe that the Bible is the inspired and inerrant Word of God, a guide for our faith and practice. We hold to the authority of Scripture as the final standard in all matters of doctrine and living.
Prayers: We begin with prayers, offering our hearts to God in worship, praise and supplication.

A cappella singing: Our worship is led through a cappella songs, as we lift our voices together in praise and adoration.

Scripture reading and preaching: The Word of God is read and expounded upon, with a sermon that seeks to apply His truth to our lives.

The Lord's Supper: Each Sunday, we partake in the Lord's Supper, remembering the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ and renewing our bond with Him.

Offerings: We give offerings as an expression of gratitude and faith, supporting the work of God's kingdom.
Children's Sunday School: Engaging lessons to help children grow in faith.

Youth groups: Bible study and fellowship for teens.

Ladies' meetings: Regular gatherings for women's fellowship and growth.

Weekly Bible studies: Opportunities to deepen understanding of Scripture.

Bible study at your residence: Home Bible study available upon request.
